Support Policy

Last Updated: 2023/11/15 13:47:22

Overview:

Paws&Tails is committed to providing exceptional support to its users, ensuring a seamless experience for pet adoption and healthcare services. This Support Policy outlines our commitment to addressing user inquiries, issues, and concerns promptly and efficiently.

Contacting Support:

Users can contact Paws&Tails Support through the following channels:

  • Email: support@pawsandtails.com
  • Phone: 9485713645
  • Contact Form: Available on the website

Support Hours:

  • Our support team operates during regular business hours, IST, from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM, on all the days.
  • Emergency support is available 24/7 for critical issues related to pet health and safety.

Response Times:

  • Paws&Tails aims to respond to support inquiries within 24 hours during regular business hours.
  • Emergency support inquiries will receive immediate attention.

Support Services:

Support services cover a range of issues, including but not limited to:

  • Technical difficulties with the website
  • Account access and management
  • Adoption process inquiries
  • Healthcare guidance for pets

Escalation Process:

  • If an issue is not resolved to the user's satisfaction, it can be escalated by contacting our Support Manager.
  • We are committed to addressing escalated issues promptly and finding satisfactory resolutions.

User Responsibilities:

  • Users are responsible for providing accurate information about their inquiries.
  • Users must comply with Paws&Tails' Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Service Limitations:

  • While we strive to provide comprehensive support, there may be limitations to the assistance we can offer, especially in cases involving external factors beyond our control.

Updates to support policies:

  • Paws&Tails may update this Support Policy as needed. Users will be notified of any significant changes.

Feedback:

  • Paws&Tails welcomes feedback on our support services. Users can provide feedback through the website or by contacting support directly.

By using Paws&Tails' support services, users agree to abide by this Support Policy. We are dedicated to continuously improving our support services to better serve the pet adoption and healthcare community.

Thank you for choosing Paws&Tails!
